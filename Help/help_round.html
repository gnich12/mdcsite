Round Trip Help

Stage 1: Trip Details

  • In service details select from the dropdown menu the Round Trip option.
  • Once the information is complete, click on the “check pricing” button.

Stage 2: Choose Vehicle

  • Here, you can choose between the available vehicle types for your request.

Stage 3: Confirm

  • You can confirm here all the information you have submitted so far.
  • Please make sure you activate the return trip by clicking the Book Return Trip? button.
  • Confirm the return date and time.
  • Complete your personal information. Make sure is correct:
    • Full Name
    • Email
    • Phone
  • You will need to create a username. You will receive further instructions by mail along with your reservation confirmation.
  • You can use your username to login in our form for future trips or login in the Book Rides Passenger Mobile App to keep track of your reservations or create new ones.
  • Note: Kids under 7 years old should use a car seat or a booster car seat.
  • Note: We can provide a better service as most accurate information we get from your trip.
