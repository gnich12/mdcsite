About the App

Book Rides Passenger App

  • We use the Book Rides service to manage our booking process.
  • The Book Rides Passenger App allows you to:
    • Create reservations and request quotes
    • Create/confirm/cancel reservations
    • Push notification support
    • Pay pending balances on reservations
    • Update your account profile and password
    • Update your billing information
    • Email yourself receipts
    • Get your current location and approximate address
    • Request meet & greet service
    • Request car seats
    • Apply Promo codes
